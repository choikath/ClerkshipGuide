The Secondary Sources


*all digital versions available in folder within same dropbox

Physical Exam: Bates' Guide to Physical Examination

qbanks

1. Go halfsies on a qbank subscription with a partner with an opposite schedule (their first half of the year has your second half blocks). One reset is included with the subscription, so you can reset answers at the halfway point.
2. Definitive source is USMLE Qbank for Step 2 (NOT Step 1) CK, 360 days.
3. during OB the department will give you a free uwise account during the rotation that includes a question bank.
4. Other questions (kaplan, pretest, etc) can supplement PRN.
5. There are 2000+ questions included. Generally found to be more helpful to make tests of 10-40 questions created from a few topics at a time rather than completely random assortment.
