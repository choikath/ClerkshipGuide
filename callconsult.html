Calling a consult

Call the number, listen to message, then after the beeps:
Enter your callback number xxx-xxx-xxxx then "#".
Three beeps means you're done and can hang up.

Operator HUP:
Operator Presby: 215-662-8000
Operator PAH:
Most hospital phones: to dial outgoing call, start with 1-____

1.   Use Penn Rolodoc as your primary guide, it will save you time waiting for the operator, and also outlines the information you'll want to have when calling a specific consult! Has both options for text-paging, or a phone number.
The operator also has the updated numbers and can connect you directly. They especially can help if you don't know exactly what department you should be calling (eg MRI - inpatient, outpatient, IR, etc).

2.   Before you call them, open up the patient's chart (vitals and labs) and signout so you can quickly access the information they may request. Typically people return calls within 5-10 minutes.

3.   Talking points:

Talking point #xx "Say this"
1. Who, What (New, F/u), Where "Hi this is _(name)_ from ____ team. I'm calling about a:
•   new consult, could I tell you about them?
•   follow-up.
The patient is XX in Room XX.
2. The Question "The question is: ...."
(ex:)
•   medication management for ___
•   recommendations for possible etiologies of ___
•   evaluation for ___ [eg osteomyelitis, renal transplant w AKI]
3. The Pitch "This is a xx yo M/F...."
•   Give a one liner
•   Give a one liner about what brought them in or what made them get admitted
•   Give the relevant labs/results/hospital course relevant to the consulting service and question.
They will guide and prompt you for what they need to know.
Keep it brief. Do your best.
4. Wrap Up "Is there anything else I can tell you, or do for you?"
•   They may tell you things they want done off the bat. Take notes and tell your intern and resident.
•   Give your number or the intern's number as a call-back #.
5. THANK YOU!!! "Thank you so much, it's very very appreciated!"

And when they call back later with recs:

6. THANK YOU!!! "<3<3"
(ex:)
•   I will name my child after you
•   You are a freaking genius
•   Thank you for being an awesome human being
